About Us

E-Cell MITAOE aims to promote entrepreneurship among young minds across India. We aim to create a robust network, through which students can come forward and share ideas among their peers and leading industrialists, by engaging in different events and competitions. We are launching our CA programme, and you can add value to it. We are looking for students from various colleges across India, who can help us expand this network, and become a part of it. We would like you to be the face of E-Cell MITAOE in your college and take this initiative forward with your voice and work to make ideas into innovation.
